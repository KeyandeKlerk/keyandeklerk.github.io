About our company

Based on the East Rand Of Johannesburg, we provide our services to Residential, Commercial & Industrial sectors. Our solid reputation is built on reliability, consistency, application & confidence that our clients will always get the most productive & cost effective outcome for their particular project. We take pride in being able to deliver a service to the building industry, that can respond to the sometimes unpredictable & dynamic challenge’s of any size construction site.

We strive to maintain our ethics policy within the company that focuses on affordability, professionalism, efficiency and most importantly service excellence. We demand the best of our staff and ourselves by always keeping J-Core and our clients values a top priority. Our aim is to be our customers’ preferred contractor by offering accurate and cost effective concrete scanning and coring solutions.
